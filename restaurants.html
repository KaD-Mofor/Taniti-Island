Restaurants in Tinati Island

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Fisherman's Grill

Fisherman's Grill is a popular waterfront restaurant offering fresh, locally caught seafood dishes. Visitors can enjoy a variety of grilled fish, shrimp, and lobster, all served with flavorful rice and tropical fruits.

Taste of America

Taste of America is a cozy restaurant that brings a taste of the United States to Tinati Island. It offers classic American dishes such as burgers, steaks, and fried chicken, along with salads and delicious desserts.
