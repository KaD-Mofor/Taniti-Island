Lodging in Taniti Island

Taniti has a wide variety of lodging that ranges from an inexpensive hostel to one large, four-star resort. There are many small, family-owned hotels and a growing number of bed and breakfasts. All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.

Four-Star Resort: Ocean Breeze Resort

Ocean Breeze Resort is a luxurious four-star resort located on the coast of Taniti Island. It offers breathtaking ocean views, spacious rooms, world-class amenities, and access to private beaches. Guests can indulge in spa treatments, gourmet dining, and various water sports activities.

Bed and Breakfast: Island Serenity

Island Serenity is a charming bed and breakfast nestled in the heart of Taniti City. Guests can experience the warm hospitality of the locals, enjoy comfortable accommodations, and savor delicious homemade breakfast. The location provides easy access to nearby attractions and cultural sites.
