Entertainment in Taniti Island

Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and visit the volcano. However, there are other things to do, including visiting a local history museum, going on chartered fishing tours, snorkeling, zip-lining in the rainforest, visiting several pubs, including a microbrewery, dancing at a new dance club, seeing a movie, taking helicopter rides, playing at an arcade, visiting art galleries, and bowling. Also, a nine-hole golf course should be operational by next year. Many of these activities are located in Merriton Landing, which is a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.

Music Concert: Tropical Beats

Tropical Beats is an annual music festival held on the beach, featuring local and international artists. It offers a vibrant atmosphere, dancing, and a variety of music genres, including reggae, pop, and electronic. It's a must-attend event for music lovers visiting Taniti Island.

Art Gallery: Island Colors

Island Colors is a contemporary art gallery that showcases the works of local artists inspired by the beauty of Taniti Island. Visitors can explore diverse art forms, including paintings, sculptures, and handcrafted items that represent the island's culture and nature.
